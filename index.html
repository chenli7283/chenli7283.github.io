About Me

Since April 2023, I have been serving as a JSPS research fellow at the Department of Complex Systems Science, Graduate School of Informatics, Nagoya University, Japan. Prior to this, I obtained both my master's and PhD in Engineering from Hiroshima University in September 2016 and September 2019, respectively. Following my doctoral studies, I worked as a researcher at the Graduate School of Advanced Science and Engineering, Hiroshima University, Japan, from October 2019 to January 2021. Subsequently, from February 2021 to March 2023, I held a research fellowship at the Department of Bioscience and Bioinformatics, Faculty of Computer Science and Systems Engineering, Kyushu Institute of Technology, Japan.
