About Me

  • 2016 - 2019: I received the PhD degree in Engineering from Hiroshima University, Japan.

  • 2019 - 2021: I was a researcher with the Graduate School of Advanced Science and Engineering, Hiroshima University, Japan.

  • 2021 - 2023: I was a research fellow with the Department of Bioscience and Bioinformatics, Faculty of Computer Science and Systems Engineering, Kyushu Institute of Technology, Japan.

  • 2023 - : I am currently a JSPS research fellow with the Department of Complex Systems Science, Graduate School of Informatics, Nagoya University, Japan.

Research Interests

I focus on using machine learning and deep learning approaches to solve practical problems.

  • Deep generative models for sequence data.

  • Prediction and forecasting for time series.

  • Data mining in distributed environment.

  • Performance evaluation for queueing systems.
